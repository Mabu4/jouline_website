About

My name is Jouline and I am a German artist who specialises in working with acrylic paints. I love bringing to the canvas what comes to my mind intuitively. I try to express myself in a versatile and unique way, therefore I wouldn't assign my art to any particular category. Each of my paintings is painted with a lot of heart and passion. Through art I feel connected to my true self and my soul. I am convinced that every human being has his own place in this world and that this gives us a chance to create a world full of peace and love.
